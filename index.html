About Me

My name is Anthony Mitchell. If you can't tell from the picture, I really enjoy golf. I've been playing the sport since the age of 13. Before I got into this program I was a greenskeeper at East Lake Golf Club. The most notable thing I did while working at ELGC, was working the final tournament of the PGA Tour, The Tour Championship.
